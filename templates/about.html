About Our Project

Welcome to our Tottenham Hotspur Star Guessing Game!

What is it?

Our project is a fun and interactive way for fans of Tottenham Hotspur to test their knowledge of the team's players. You'll have the opportunity to guess the star Tottenham players, and when you guess correctly, it will take you to a fan page dedicated to that player.

How does it work?

  1. Guessing Game: You'll be presented with images or clues of Tottenham Hotspur players.
  2. Guess the Player: Use your knowledge and intuition to guess the player's name.
  3. Get Redirected: When you guess correctly, you'll be redirected to a fan page filled with information, stats, and highlights of that player.
